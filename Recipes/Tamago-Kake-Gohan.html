Tamago Kake Gohan - Silver Spoon

Ingredients

  • 1 pasteurised egg
  • 1 cup of rice
  • Soy sauce

Optional

  • Miso soup on the side
  • Furikake (Also known as rice seasoning)

Directions

  1. Prepare a bowl of rice
  2. Crack the egg on top of the rice
  3. Drizzle a small amount of soy sauce
  4. Mix well and enjoy!
